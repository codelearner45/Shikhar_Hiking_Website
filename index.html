About Shikhar

"Choose Shikhar for an unforgettable adventure experience with a focus on safety, authenticity, and the thrill of exploration."

Experienced Guides:

Our team of seasoned guides knows the terrain and ensures your safety at every step.

No-Waste Policy:

We practice a strict "leave no trace" approach, ensuring that all waste is managed responsibly. Our guides carry out all non-biodegradable waste to keep trails clean and pristine.

High-Quality Gear:

We provide top-of-the-line equipment to ensure comfort and safety on all treks.

Community Involvement:

Shikhar actively collaborates with local communities, supporting cultural preservation and fostering mutually beneficial relationships.

Authentic Experiences:

We offer immersive, culturally rich journeys that bring you closer to the heart of the mountains.
